for the friend who never stopped mattering to me

I know it's been a while. Longer than it should have been. Life got loud, days folded into each other, and somewhere in that noise I let the space between us grow quiet — not because I stopped caring, but because I let everything else get louder than you deserved.

You've always been the kind of friend who shows up fully, who checks in without being asked, who makes the ordinary feel a little lighter. I don't say this enough, so let me say it plainly now: I'm sorry for the silence, and I'm grateful it hasn't changed how much I care about you.

Nothing about how I feel toward you has ever gone away. Not for a single day. I just went quiet — and you deserved better than that.

— always your friend, Tylor (aka "Madara Uchiha", when you're feeling dramatic)
